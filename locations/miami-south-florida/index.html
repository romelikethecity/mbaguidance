The Miami & South Florida MBA Job Market

Miami has evolved from a vacation destination into a finance and international business hub. Citadel relocated its HQ here. Private equity, hedge funds, and wealth management firms are multiplying. Real estate development drives significant MBA employment. Miami's Latin American business connections are unmatched in the US.

Cost of Living

Rising rapidly. Rent in Coral Gables or Brickell runs $2,000-$3,200/month. Miami is no longer cheap, but it's still below NYC. No state income tax in Florida adds significant value to your post-MBA take-home pay.

Why Get an MBA in Miami & South Florida

If Latin American business or international commerce is your goal, Miami is the US gateway. Miami Herbert (#59) provides direct access to Citadel, Blackstone's Florida operations, and a growing ecosystem of relocated financial firms. No state income tax. The lifestyle (weather, culture, beach) is a real quality-of-life advantage.

What to Watch Out For

Miami's corporate market is growing but still emerging compared to NYC, Chicago, or the Bay Area. For traditional consulting or Big Tech, you'll recruit nationally. The city's rapid growth has driven up costs without proportional salary increases in some sectors.

Frequently Asked Questions

What MBA programs are in South Florida?

University of Miami Herbert (#59) in Coral Gables is the prominent South Florida MBA. Florida International University also offers an MBA but is lower-ranked. Herbert is the pick for South Florida careers.

Is Miami good for finance MBA careers?

Improving rapidly. Citadel, Point72, and several PE firms have relocated or expanded in Miami. The wealth management sector is growing. Miami isn't NYC, but it's the fastest-growing financial center in the US. No state income tax makes compensation go further.

How much does Miami Herbert cost?

Tuition is approximately $55,000/year. Coral Gables housing adds $24,000-$36,000/year. The total investment for two years is roughly $180,000-$200,000 before scholarships for 2026.
