The Denver-Boulder MBA Job Market

Denver's economy has diversified from energy into tech, healthcare, and aerospace. Arrow Electronics, DaVita, Western Union, and Lockheed Martin are major employers. Boulder has a concentrated startup scene and is the hub of the natural products industry (Celestial Seasonings, organic brands). Google has a growing Boulder office.

Cost of Living

Moderate to high. Denver rent runs $1,500-$2,200/month. Boulder is slightly more expensive. Both are below coastal prices but have climbed in the past five years. The outdoor lifestyle (300+ days of sunshine, skiing, hiking) is the payoff.

Why Get an MBA in Denver-Boulder

If outdoor lifestyle matters as much as career outcomes, Colorado is hard to beat. CU Leeds (#60) in Boulder provides a flagship university MBA with startup ecosystem access. DU Daniels (#83) in Denver puts you in the state's business capital. Both programs offer in-state value for Colorado residents.

What to Watch Out For

The Colorado MBA job market is growing but still smaller than TX, CA, or East Coast metros. Most graduates who want big-company careers recruit nationally. Altitude takes adjustment (6,000+ feet). Housing costs have risen sharply.

Frequently Asked Questions

What MBA programs are in Colorado?

CU Boulder Leeds (#60) and DU Denver Daniels (#83). Leeds is the higher-ranked program at a public university. Daniels offers a private university experience in Denver's urban center. Both produce strong Colorado-based outcomes.

Is Denver good for tech careers after an MBA?

Growing. Denver's tech scene has expanded with remote-work migration and companies like Palantir establishing offices. It's not SF or Austin, but the tech-to-cost-of-living ratio is improving. Both Leeds and Daniels graduates find local tech roles.

How much do Colorado MBA programs cost?

Leeds (CU in-state) is approximately $22,000/year. Daniels (DU private) is approximately $55,000/year. For Colorado residents, Leeds is one of the best values in the state. Daniels offers merit scholarships that can reduce the gap.
