How Deloitte Strategy hires MBAs

Monitor Deloitte (now Deloitte Strategy) hires more MBAs than any tier 2 consulting firm and competes directly with MBB on prestige work in select industries. The acquisition of Monitor in 2013 elevated Deloitte's strategy practice from a Big Four advisory arm to a credible MBB alternative.

Deloitte recruits more broadly than MBB, with on-campus presence at top-25 programs and off-campus access from top-50. The firm hires across multiple practices (Strategy, Customer & Marketing, Mergers & Acquisitions), so a candidate cut from the Strategy track can sometimes redirect to another practice.

Interview process

Two rounds of case interviews plus behavioral interviews. The cases emphasize industry-specific problems (healthcare, financial services, tech). Practice-area knowledge matters more than at MBB, where firms hire generalist Associates first.

Deloitte Strategy compensation breakdown

First-year total compensation at Deloitte Strategy runs about $225K for US-based MBA hires. The breakdown: $175K base, $25K signing bonus paid at start, $25K performance bonus or stock vest in the first year.

Year-two compensation typically grows 10-15%, with promotion to Senior Associate or equivalent in years 2-3 producing larger jumps. Top performers in Consulting can clear $400K total comp by year 4-5.

What MBA programs does Deloitte Strategy hire from?

Deloitte Strategy recruits hardest at Harvard Business School, Wharton, and Booth, with significant on-campus presence at the rest of the M7 plus the top 15. Off-campus hires from outside this list are possible but require deeper networking and stronger backgrounds.

What's the starting salary at Deloitte Strategy for MBAs?

First-year compensation at Deloitte Strategy runs $175K base, $25K signing bonus, and $25K performance bonus, totaling roughly $225K. RSU grants and other variable compensation can shift the total significantly for tech firms.

How hard is it to get hired by Deloitte Strategy after an MBA?

Deloitte Strategy is a Tier 2 firm in Consulting, which puts it in the top tier of MBA selectivity. Even at target schools, only 10-30% of applicants typically receive offers. Most successful candidates come in with relevant pre-MBA experience and strong campus interview performance.

What's the Deloitte Strategy interview process like?

Two rounds of case interviews plus behavioral interviews. The cases emphasize industry-specific problems (healthcare, financial services, tech). Practice-area knowledge matters more than at MBB, where firms hire generalist Associates first.
