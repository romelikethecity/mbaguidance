Why People Compare These Two

Goizueta (#21) and Fuqua (#11) are the two best MBA programs in the Southeast. Candidates choosing between them are weighing Fuqua's stronger ranking and brand against Goizueta's Atlanta location, smaller class, and competitive scholarship offers. Many applicants to one apply to the other.

Career Markets

Atlanta has 16 Fortune 500 headquarters including Coca-Cola, Delta, Home Depot, and UPS. Durham has Duke Health and Research Triangle Park. For local job markets, Atlanta wins on corporate density. Fuqua compensates with a national brand that recruits broadly across industries. Both place well into consulting.

Cost and ROI

Goizueta's smaller class (190 students) often translates to more scholarship dollars per student. Atlanta's cost of living is moderate. Durham is cheaper still. Both programs offer strong ROI, but Goizueta's scholarship generosity can make the total investment significantly lower than Fuqua's sticker price.

The Honest Take

Fuqua's 10-spot ranking advantage is meaningful for competitive national recruiting. If you're gunning for MBB consulting or top-tier PE, Fuqua's brand gives you an edge. If you plan to work in Atlanta, in healthcare, or if Goizueta offers a scholarship that Fuqua doesn't match, Emory becomes the better value play. Visit both and compare the cultures.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is the ranking difference between Fuqua and Goizueta significant?

The 10-spot gap (#11 vs #21) matters for careers where prestige is a filter (PE, VC, top consulting). For corporate careers, healthcare, and roles where experience matters more than pedigree, the gap narrows. Both are well-respected programs.

Which is better for healthcare?

Both are strong in healthcare. Fuqua benefits from Duke Health, one of the top hospital systems in the country. Emory benefits from the CDC and Emory Healthcare. For healthcare consulting or pharma, either school is a strong choice.

Which offers more scholarships?

Goizueta tends to be more generous with merit scholarships due to its smaller class size. Fuqua offers scholarships too, but the competition for them is stiffer. Compare your specific offers.
