Why People Compare These Two

Both are affordable MBA programs in the Deep South. Alabama Manderson (#87) has the higher ranking and SEC brand. Memphis Fogelman (#126) has the FedEx supply chain pipeline and Tennessee's no-income-tax advantage. Manderson produces better overall career outcomes, but Memphis wins specifically for supply chain and logistics careers.

The Honest Take

Manderson is the better MBA overall. Stronger ranking, broader placement, bigger alumni network. Memphis wins only if supply chain is your career target. The FedEx headquarters advantage is real and specific. For everything else, Alabama's SEC brand and career services edge justify the slightly higher cost.
