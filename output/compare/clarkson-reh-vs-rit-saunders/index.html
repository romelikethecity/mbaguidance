Why People Compare These Two

Both are upstate New York engineering university MBAs. Clarkson ($38K total for one year) in Potsdam and RIT ($48K/year) in Rochester both give graduates technical credibility. Clarkson is faster (one year). RIT has a larger employer base (Rochester vs Potsdam). The format and location drive the choice.

The Honest Take

RIT wins on employer proximity: Rochester has Xerox, Paychex, and a growing tech sector. Clarkson wins on speed: one year vs two. RIT's salary edge ($82K vs $71K) reflects the Rochester market advantage. Clarkson's total cost ($38K) is lower than two years at RIT ($96K). For engineers who want the fastest path to an MBA, Clarkson delivers. For students who want more time and a better local job market, RIT is the choice.

Frequently Asked Questions

Which is faster?

Clarkson's one-year format finishes in 12 months. RIT's program takes two years. Clarkson saves a full year of opportunity cost.

Which has better salary outcomes?

RIT ($82K) outearns Clarkson ($71K). Rochester's larger employer base drives higher salaries.

Which city has more employers?

Rochester is significantly larger than Potsdam. RIT benefits from proximity to Xerox, Paychex, and the Rochester tech ecosystem. Potsdam has minimal local employers.
