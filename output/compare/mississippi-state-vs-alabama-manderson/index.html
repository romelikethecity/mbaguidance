Why People Compare These Two

Both are SEC MBA programs in the deep South. Alabama Manderson (#87) in Tuscaloosa has the higher ranking and the Alabama brand. Mississippi State (#130) in Starkville is cheaper and sits in one of the lowest cost-of-living areas in the US. Manderson outranks Mississippi State by 43 positions and produces higher salaries.

The Honest Take

Manderson is the better MBA. Higher ranking, stronger brand, better career outcomes. Mississippi State wins on cost: Starkville's cost of living is among the lowest of any college town in America, and in-state tuition is minimal. For students who will work in Mississippi and want the cheapest possible credential, Mississippi State delivers. For anything beyond the immediate region, Alabama's brand is significantly stronger.
