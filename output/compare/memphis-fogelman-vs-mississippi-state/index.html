Why People Compare These Two

Both are affordable Mid-South MBAs with strong ROI. Memphis ($12.3K/year) has FedEx and supply chain strength. Mississippi State ($12.3K/year) has a 22-person class and project management focus. Both produce $92K average salaries at nearly identical tuition. This is a rare comparison where both programs deserve equal consideration.

The Honest Take

The numbers are almost identical: same tuition, same salary. Memphis wins for supply chain careers (FedEx hub is unmatched). Mississippi State wins for class intimacy (22 students vs 35) and the college-town experience. Both are exceptional values. The decision is career direction (supply chain vs general management) and lifestyle (Memphis vs Starkville).

Frequently Asked Questions

Which is a better value?

Both cost approximately $12,300/year and produce $92K average salaries. The ROI is essentially identical and among the best in MBA education.

Which is better for supply chain?

Memphis Fogelman's proximity to FedEx's global superhub makes it the clear choice for supply chain and logistics careers.

Which has a smaller class?

Mississippi State enrolls about 22 students; Memphis enrolls about 35. Mississippi State offers a more intimate cohort experience.
