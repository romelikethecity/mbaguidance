Why People Compare These Two

Both are South Florida MBA programs serving different markets. Miami Herbert (#46) is a top-50 program with international business strength and Coral Gables prestige. FAU (#136) in Boca Raton offers affordable public tuition with finance and real estate programs in Palm Beach County. The ranking gap (90 positions) is massive.

The Honest Take

Herbert is the dramatically better MBA. Top-50 ranking, strong international brand, and significantly better career outcomes. FAU wins on cost and Palm Beach County real estate/wealth management connections. For students targeting Palm Beach County finance careers at minimal cost, FAU provides relevant employer access. For national or international career ambitions, Herbert is worth the premium.
