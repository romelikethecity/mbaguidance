Why People Compare These Two

Chicago's two mid-tier Catholic MBA programs are blocks apart in the Loop. DePaul Kellstadt (#95) has the higher ranking and strong marketing and real estate programs. Loyola Chicago Quinlan (#108) offers Jesuit values-based education with healthcare management emphasis. Both provide evening and weekend options for working professionals. Both access the same Chicago employer market.

The Honest Take

DePaul has the ranking edge and broader program offerings (marketing, real estate, finance). Loyola wins for healthcare management and students who value Jesuit educational philosophy. Since both schools are in downtown Chicago, the city access is identical. Choose based on specialization: marketing and real estate at DePaul, healthcare and ethics at Loyola.
