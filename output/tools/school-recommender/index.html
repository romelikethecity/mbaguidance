How the recommender works

The matching algorithm scores each MBA program against your inputs across six dimensions: industry placement strength, geography, class size fit, GMAT compatibility, career-changer friendliness, and tuition fit. The 7 highest-scoring programs become your shortlist.

School data comes from each program's most recent class profile and employment report. The matching weights are calibrated against the MBA admissions patterns we've watched succeed and fail across the past five admissions cycles.

This isn't an admit predictor

The recommender tells you where you should apply based on fit. It doesn't tell you your odds at each program. Those are different questions. For your odds, try the MBA Admit Predictor. For deep school comparisons, browse the comparison library or the Class of 2026 Hub.
