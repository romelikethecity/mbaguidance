The Portland-Eugene MBA Job Market

Portland's economy is anchored by Nike, Adidas North America, Columbia Sportswear, and Intel. The city's sustainability focus attracts B Corps and clean tech companies. Portland's tech scene includes small to mid-size companies and a growing startup ecosystem. Eugene (home of UO) is a smaller market, but Portland is 110 miles north.

Cost of Living

Moderate. Portland rent runs $1,400-$2,000/month. Eugene is cheaper at $1,000-$1,500. Oregon has no sales tax, which helps with day-to-day expenses. Portland's cost of living has risen but remains well below SF or Seattle.

Why Get an MBA in Portland-Eugene

If sportswear, sustainability, or Pacific Northwest lifestyle matters, Oregon Lundquist (#82) in Eugene provides a pathway. Nike, Adidas, and Columbia Sportswear recruit from UO. The program is affordable for Oregon residents, and Eugene's low cost of living keeps total MBA costs down.

What to Watch Out For

Eugene is a small college town with limited local corporate employment. Most career opportunities require relocating to Portland or recruiting nationally. Oregon's economy is smaller than Washington or California. Rain. Lots of rain (9 months of it in Eugene).

Frequently Asked Questions

What MBA programs are in Oregon?

Oregon Lundquist (#82) in Eugene is the state's primary MBA program. Portland State also offers an MBA but is lower-ranked. For Oregon-based careers, Lundquist is the pick.

Can I work for Nike with an Oregon MBA?

Nike recruits from UO Lundquist and the relationship is real. Oregon athletes wear Nike, and the university-brand connection goes deep. That said, Nike also recruits from many top MBA programs nationally. Being at Lundquist gives you proximity and network, not a guaranteed path.

Is Portland a good MBA market?

For sportswear and sustainability, it's unique. For tech, it's growing but still smaller than Seattle, SF, or LA. Portland's quality of life is high, but the job market is more niche than broad. Most MBA graduates target Portland or Seattle for post-MBA careers.
