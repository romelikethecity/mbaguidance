Elon (Love) graduates earn an average starting salary of $83,000 with an employment rate of 84% within three months of graduation. These numbers reflect solid outcomes with room for growth as the program matures.

Industry Breakdown

Elon (Love)'s employment report shows graduates entering these industries:

  • Consulting: 25-35%
  • Finance: 15-25%
  • Technology: 15-25%
  • Other: 15-25% (healthcare, corporate, non-profit, entrepreneurship)

Elon (Love) is particularly strong for careers in Hands-On Learners, North Carolina Careers, Teaching-Focused. The school's full profile details specific employer relationships and career pipelines.

Salary by Industry

Average starting salaries vary significantly by industry:

  • Consulting: $175,000-$190,000 base + $25,000-$35,000 signing bonus
  • Finance (banking): $175,000 base + $50,000-$65,000 signing bonus
  • Technology: $150,000-$180,000 base + equity/RSUs
  • Corporate (Fortune 500): $130,000-$155,000 base + $15,000-$25,000 signing bonus

The $83,000 average at Elon (Love) is a blend across all industries. Students entering consulting and finance typically exceed this average, while those entering non-profit or government roles may earn below it. Career Services and Recruiting

Elon (Love)'s career services team connects students with employer presentations, networking events, career treks, and alumni mentorship. The 84% employment rate reflects the program's ability to place graduates effectively.

When evaluating employment reports, look beyond the headline salary number. Consider:

  • What percentage of students enter their target industry?
  • How active is the alumni network in your desired field?
  • What career-change support does the school provide?

Elon (Love) Employment: Quick Answers

What's the average salary for Elon (Love) MBA graduates?

Elon (Love) graduates earn a median base salary of $83,000, with signing bonuses typically adding $25,000-$35,000. Total first-year compensation lands in the $108K-$128K range depending on industry and role.

What's the employment rate at Elon (Love)?

84% of Elon (Love) graduates were employed within three months of graduation. The remainder are typically founders, candidates pursuing additional credentials, or those still actively recruiting.

What industries do Elon (Love) grads enter?

Elon (Love)'s strongest placement industries are Teaching Quality, Experiential Learning, Faculty Mentoring. Industry mix varies by class, with consulting and finance generally leading and technology growing year over year.

What percentage of Elon (Love) grads go into consulting?

Consulting is among Elon (Love)'s top employment outcomes, with placement rates at top programs ranging from 25% to 35% depending on the year. McKinsey, BCG, and Bain are perennial top employers, joined by Big Four strategy practices and boutique firms.
