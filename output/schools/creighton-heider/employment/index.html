Creighton Heider graduates earn an average starting salary of $80,000 with an employment rate of 87% within three months of graduation. These numbers reflect solid outcomes with room for growth as the program matures.

Industry Breakdown

Creighton Heider's employment report shows graduates entering these industries:

  • Finance: 25-35% (banking, PE, hedge funds, asset management)
  • Consulting: 20-30% (MBB, Big 4, boutique)
  • Technology: 15-25% (product, strategy, operations)
  • Other: 10-20% (corporate, healthcare, non-profit)

Creighton Heider is particularly strong for careers in Values-Driven Careers, Omaha/Midwest Careers, Finance Professionals. The school's full profile details specific employer relationships and career pipelines.

Salary by Industry

Average starting salaries vary significantly by industry:

  • Consulting: $175,000-$190,000 base + $25,000-$35,000 signing bonus
  • Finance (banking): $175,000 base + $50,000-$65,000 signing bonus
  • Technology: $150,000-$180,000 base + equity/RSUs
  • Corporate (Fortune 500): $130,000-$155,000 base + $15,000-$25,000 signing bonus

The $80,000 average at Creighton Heider is a blend across all industries. Students entering consulting and finance typically exceed this average, while those entering non-profit or government roles may earn below it. Career Services and Recruiting

Creighton Heider's career services team connects students with employer presentations, networking events, career treks, and alumni mentorship. The 87% employment rate reflects the program's ability to place graduates effectively.

Creighton Heider Employment: Quick Answers

What's the average salary for Creighton Heider MBA graduates?

Creighton Heider graduates earn a median base salary of $80,000, with signing bonuses typically adding $25,000-$35,000. Total first-year compensation lands in the $105K-$125K range depending on industry and role.

What's the employment rate at Creighton Heider?

87% of Creighton Heider graduates were employed within three months of graduation. The remainder are typically founders, candidates pursuing additional credentials, or those still actively recruiting.

What industries do Creighton Heider grads enter?

Creighton Heider's strongest placement industries are Ethical Leadership, Finance, General Management. Industry mix varies by class, with consulting and finance generally leading and technology growing year over year.

What percentage of Creighton Heider grads go into consulting?

Consulting is among Creighton Heider's top employment outcomes, with placement rates at top programs ranging from 25% to 35% depending on the year. McKinsey, BCG, and Bain are perennial top employers, joined by Big Four strategy practices and boutique firms.
