Which Round Should You Apply?

Round 1 is the optimal time to apply. Admissions committees review applications with the most available seats, the deepest scholarship pools, and fresh energy. Roughly 40-50% of the class is admitted in Round 1 at most programs.

Round 2 is still competitive. If your GMAT score, essays, or recommender letters aren't ready by Round 1, a strong Round 2 application is better than a rushed Round 1 submission. About 40% of admits come from Round 2.

Round 3 is viable for exceptional candidates only. Most seats are filled, and the bar is higher. Apply Round 3 only if your profile is unusually strong or if you're applying to programs ranked outside the top 15.

Columbia Business School Application Timing: Quick Answers

When are Columbia Business School's MBA application deadlines for 2026?

Columbia Business School runs three application rounds for fall 2026 entry. Round 1 deadlines fall in September-October, Round 2 in January, and Round 3 in March-April. Check the official Columbia Business School site for the exact dates each cycle.

Should I apply Round 1 or Round 2 to Columbia Business School?

Round 1 is statistically stronger at Columbia Business School: more open seats, deeper scholarship pools, and admissions committees reviewing applications with fresh attention. Most schools admit 40-50% of the class in Round 1. Apply Round 2 only if your GMAT, essays, or recommenders aren't ready by Round 1.

Is it worth applying Round 3 at Columbia Business School?

At Columbia Business School's ranking tier, Round 3 is difficult. Most seats are already filled. Consider Round 3 only if your profile is exceptionally strong or you have a distinctive background that fills a gap in the admitted class. For most candidates, applying Round 1 next cycle is a better bet.

How long does Columbia Business School take to make admissions decisions?

Columbia Business School typically issues decisions 8-12 weeks after the round deadline. Round 1 decisions land in December, Round 2 in March-April, Round 3 in May. Interview invitations usually arrive 4-6 weeks after submission.
