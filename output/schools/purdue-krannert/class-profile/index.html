Purdue Krannert's class of 90 students represents a small, intimate community. The smaller class creates tight-knit relationships and a community where you'll know most of your classmates by name.

Academic Profile

The average GMAT score is 680, with the middle 80% typically ranging from 650 to 700. The average GPA is 3.25. These are averages, meaning roughly half the admitted class scored below these numbers. Strong work experience, compelling essays, and demonstrated leadership can offset below-average test scores.

Most admitted students have 3-7 years of work experience, with an average of approximately 5 years. The admissions committee looks for candidates who've had enough professional experience to contribute meaningfully to classroom discussions but are early enough in their careers to benefit from the MBA's career-changing potential.

Professional Background

Purdue Krannert attracts students from diverse professional backgrounds. Common pre-MBA industries include consulting, financial services, technology, and non-profit/government. The school particularly values candidates whose backgrounds align with its strengths in STEM MBA, Operations, Analytics, Manufacturing.

Diversity of background is a deliberate admissions strategy. Schools build classes where a former investment banker sits next to a Peace Corps volunteer sits next to a software engineer. This diversity is the source of the MBA classroom's value.

Culture and Community at Purdue Krannert

Krannert's 90-person class is intimate and collaborative. West Lafayette is a small college town, which means the MBA community is self-contained and close. Students with engineering backgrounds feel at home in Purdue's analytical culture. The cost of living is among the lowest of any MBA program, which means student budgets stretch further here than almost anywhere.

What This Means for Applicants

Understanding the class profile helps you position your application. If your GMAT is below 680, consider retaking or emphasizing other strengths. If you come from an over-represented background (consulting, banking), you'll need to differentiate on leadership, personal story, and school fit. If you come from an under-represented background (military, non-profit, healthcare), that diversity is an asset.

The class profile also tells you what your learning environment will look like. A class of 90 with these academic credentials means a collaborative, supportive environment that values practical experience.

Purdue Krannert MBA Class of 2026: Class Size and Average Work Experience Summary
