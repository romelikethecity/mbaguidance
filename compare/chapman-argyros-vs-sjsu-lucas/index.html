Why People Compare These Two

Both are California MBA programs serving different ecosystems. Chapman ($52K/year) in Orange County connects to the entertainment industry. SJSU ($16K/year) in San Jose connects to Silicon Valley. The tuition gap is massive ($52K vs $16K), and SJSU produces higher salaries ($91K vs $76K).

The Honest Take

For pure ROI, SJSU wins by a wide margin: higher salary ($91K vs $76K) at one-third the tuition. Chapman wins only for entertainment industry careers, where the MBA/MFA dual degree and film school crossover create unique value. For any non-entertainment career goal, SJSU is the rational California MBA choice.

Frequently Asked Questions

Which is more affordable?

SJSU ($16K/year for CA residents) is dramatically cheaper than Chapman ($52K/year). The tuition gap is the largest in this comparison set.

Which is better for entertainment?

Chapman's MBA/MFA dual degree and Dodge College film school connection create entertainment industry opportunities that SJSU doesn't offer.

Which has better salary outcomes?

SJSU ($91K) outearns Chapman ($76K). Silicon Valley's tech salaries drive SJSU's higher outcomes.
