Why People Compare These Two

Both are small private MBA programs in Southern California. LMU (#85) on LA's Westside has the higher ranking and Jesuit values-based education. Chapman Argyros (#127) in Orange has entrepreneurship focus and entertainment industry connections through Chapman's film school. LMU outranks Chapman by 42 positions.

The Honest Take

LMU has the ranking advantage and stronger LA employer connections through its Westside location. Chapman wins for entertainment industry careers where the film school crossover creates unique MBA/MFA dual-degree opportunities. Both are pricey private California MBAs. For general LA business careers, LMU is the better choice. For entertainment and entrepreneurship, Chapman has distinctive value.
