Why People Compare These Two

These are Kentucky's two flagship MBA programs. Kentucky Gatton (#64) in Lexington outranks Louisville (#119) by 55 spots. Both are affordable. Gatton produces higher average salaries and has stronger national visibility. Louisville wins on healthcare employer access, with Humana and UPS headquarters in the city.

The Honest Take

Gatton is the better MBA on paper and by outcomes. If you want to stay in Kentucky and work in healthcare administration or logistics, Louisville's location advantage is real. For anything else, Gatton's higher ranking and stronger career placement make it the rational choice. The tuition difference is minimal between the two.

Frequently Asked Questions

Which is higher ranked?

Kentucky Gatton (#64) outranks Louisville (#119) by a significant margin.

Which is better for healthcare?

Louisville has the edge in healthcare because of Humana's headquarters and UofL Health. Gatton has healthcare connections too, but Louisville's are more concentrated.

Which has better salary outcomes?

Gatton produces higher average salaries, reflecting its stronger national placement. Louisville salaries are solid for the regional market.
