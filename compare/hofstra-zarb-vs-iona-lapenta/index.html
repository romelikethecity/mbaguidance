Why People Compare These Two

Both are small NYC metro MBA programs north of Manhattan. Hofstra Zarb (#123) on Long Island and Iona LaPenta (#149) in New Rochelle serve suburban working professionals who want an MBA without the Manhattan commute. Hofstra has the higher ranking and larger program. Iona offers smaller cohorts and Westchester location.

The Honest Take

Hofstra has the ranking advantage and broader program. Iona wins on class size intimacy and Westchester accessibility for residents north of the city. Neither has strong brand recognition beyond the NYC metro region. Both are practical choices for working professionals who want a nearby, affordable MBA credential. Hofstra is the stronger pick on paper; Iona is the better fit for Westchester-based students.
